The Huracán's name (huracán being the Spanish word for hurricane) is inspired by a Spanish fighting bull. The use of names from historical Spanish fighting bulls have been the traditional naming scheme of most Lamborghini car models. Huracán was a bull known for its courage that fought in 1879.[4] Also Huracan is the Mayan god of wind, storm and fire.[5]
